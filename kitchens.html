Kitchens

Kitchens are spaces within a home that are designed for food preparation, cooking, and eating. They typically include essential appliances like stoves, ovens, refrigerators, and sinks, as well as cabinetry for storage. Kitchens can range from small and simple to large and luxurious, depending on the size and style of the home.
